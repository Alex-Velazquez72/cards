Hiriluk

Dr. Hiriluk was a healer and the adoptive father of Chopper. He rescued Chopper and took him to live with him. He was always chasing a dream that took him many years alone, until Chopper came. Thousands of explosions happened in his home, destroying it, but everything was for the sake of completing his research. His last words before he died were: “When do you think a person dies? When a bullet hits their heart? No. When they have an incurable disease? No. When they eat soup with poisonous mushrooms? NO! A person dies when they are forgotten...”

Gol D. Roger

Gol D. Roger, also known as Gold Roger, was the captain of the Roger Pirates and was known as the King of the Pirates. He got this title after conquering the Grand Line and owning the legendary treasure called One Piece, being the first with his crew to reach Laugh Tale in the last eight hundred years. His last words were: "Wealth, fame, power... The man who got everything in this world, the King of the Pirates 'Gold Roger.' The last words he said just before he died sent many people to the seas."

Donquixote Rosinante

Donquixote Rosinante was a former World Noble from the Donquixote family. He was the second son of Donquixote Homing and the second person under the codename Corazon. After his family gave up their status as Celestial Dragons, he became a pirate. Rosinante became the Supreme Officer of the Donquixote Pirates, working under his older brother, Donquixote Doflamingo. His last words before he died to Law were: "Walk, Law. Stay unnoticed... quietly... go far, very, very far, let nothing tie you down... not the iron borders of 'White City'... not a shortened life. Kozuki Oden

Kozuki Oden was the daimyo of Kuri in Wano Country and the father of Kozuki Momonosuke and Kozuki Hiyori. He was also the son of the former shogun of Wano Country, Kozuki Sukiyaki. Oden was the former commander of the 2nd division of the Whitebeard Pirates and a member of the Roger Pirates, where he discovered Joy Boy's treasure when he reached Laugh Tale. His last words before he died were: "I am Oden! And I was born to boil!"

Portgas D. Ace

Portgas D. Ace, born as Gol D. Ace and nicknamed Ace, was the sworn older brother of Luffy and Sabo. He was the biological son of the late King of the Pirates, Gol D. Roger, and Portgas D. Rouge. Ace was adopted by Monkey D. Garp, as Roger asked before his execution. He was also the commander of the 2nd division of the Whitebeard Pirates and the former captain of the Spade Pirates. His last words before he died were: "Well... if it weren't for Sabo... and for having a little brother like you... I wouldn’t have wanted to live..."

Edward Newgate

Edward Newgate, better known as Whitebeard, was the captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and the historic rival of Gol D. Roger. After Roger's death, he was known as the man closest to the One Piece and recognized as the strongest man in the world. In the past, before forming his own crew, he was a member of the legendary Rocks Pirates, and later in life, he became one of the Four Emperors who ruled the New World until his death in the Battle of Marineford two years ago. His last words before he died were: "The One Piece... exists!"
